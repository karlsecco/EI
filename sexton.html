The Sexton is an unexpected modern-day irish single malt for the everyman.

Made from 100% irish malted barley, The Sexton is triple distilled in copper pot stills and aged in former Oloroso sherry casks.

The result is a perfect balance of rich, dried fruits and subtle oak notes with a smooth finish that will leave you wanting more.
